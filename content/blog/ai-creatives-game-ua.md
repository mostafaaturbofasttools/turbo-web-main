The winning creative in game UA is rarely the first draft. It is the tenth variation — tested, trimmed, and tuned for each channel.

## AI speeds up iteration

Generative tools produce hook variants, thumbnail frames, and copy drafts in minutes. That volume matters when CPI pressure is high and platforms reward fresh creative.

## Humans protect quality

Editors catch off-brand moments, awkward hands in AI art, and hooks that click in a spreadsheet but fail in a 15-second video. Our team runs AI output through human review before anything hits live campaigns.

## What we measure

Install rate, D1 retention by creative cohort, and payback windows — not vanity views. Creatives that bring the wrong audience die fast, even if CTR looks great.

## Built into TRBO’s platform

Data-driven creatives are a core service, not an add-on. AI and human teams work hand in hand to find the best value for each title we publish.
