AI is reshaping mobile game development, but the teams winning in 2026 are not “AI-only.” They pair generative tools with human judgment at every step.

## Where AI helps most

**Concept and market research.** Scanning genre trends, competitor features, and store reviews faster than a manual sweep.

**Prototyping.** Grey-box levels, placeholder art, and balance spreadsheets that humans refine before production.

**Live ops.** Segmenting players, surfacing anomaly alerts, and drafting event copy that designers edit before ship.

## Where humans still lead

Fun is not fully automatable. Game feel, pacing, and the emotional beat of a tutorial need a designer’s eye. Our in-house and co-dev teams use AI to move faster — not to skip the playtest.

## TRBO’s internal platform

Our tooling blends AI automation with human-in-the-loop review. That is how we support more titles across publishing, analytics, and creative testing without losing quality.
