Getting your app published is no longer just about shipping to the store. Distribution across paid and organic channels determines whether a great product finds its audience.

## What publishers look for

Strong apps show healthy retention curves. During review we look at **D1, D7, and D28 retention**, recent revenue, and MAU or download trends. A clear category fit and a team that can iterate quickly matter as much as polish. You do not need all of that on day one — we ask for detailed metrics after the initial submission.

## Self-publish vs partner publishing

Some apps launch under TRBO directly. Others reach scale faster when we work with partners like Supercent, Voodoo, or Homa to publish and distribute. The right path depends on your metrics, genre, and growth goals.

## Hybrid growth wins

Paid UA alone is expensive. Organic ASO, creative testing, and data-driven campaigns together create sustainable install growth. That is why TRBO invests in both channels.

## Ready to submit?

If you have an app worth publishing, [submit your project](/publish) with your app details, stage, and links (website, App Store, or Google Play). We assess every submission carefully and follow up for metrics when there is a fit.
