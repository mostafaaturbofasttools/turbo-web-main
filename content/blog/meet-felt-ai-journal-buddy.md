**Felt – AI Journal Buddy** is our flagship wellness app — a privacy-first space for journaling, mood tracking, and daily affirmations, guided by **Mentora**, your AI coach.

## Why we built Felt

Mental wellbeing apps should feel personal, not generic. Felt combines guided journaling, voice entries, and affirmations that adapt to your mood — all in one calm, beautiful experience.

## Mentora, your AI buddy

Write or speak your thoughts and Mentora reflects patterns, offers CBT-inspired prompts, and suggests small next steps. It is support, not judgment. The more you journal, the more personal the guidance becomes.

## Privacy-first by design

Your data is encrypted and stored securely. Felt is built for everyday stress and self-care — not as a replacement for clinical care.
