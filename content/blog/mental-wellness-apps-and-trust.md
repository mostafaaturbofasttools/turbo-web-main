The best mental wellness apps do not chase viral moments. They earn daily habits through trust, consistency, and design that respects the user.

## Small daily rituals matter

Journaling for two minutes, a single affirmation, or a mood check-in can shift how someone starts their day. Apps that reduce friction — voice entry, lock screen widgets, gentle reminders — win retention.

## Privacy is non-negotiable

Users share vulnerable thoughts. Transparent privacy practices, clear data policies, and encryption are table stakes. Without trust, even the smartest AI features fall flat.

## AI should feel supportive

AI in wellness works when it listens and reflects — not when it diagnoses or overwhelms. Thoughtful prompts and pattern recognition help users see themselves more clearly over time.

## Felt as an example

We applied these principles to [Felt](/felt): Mentora guides without judging, affirmations adapt to context, and privacy stays at the center. If you are building in this space, lead with trust — the metrics will follow.
